Our Client

  • Client Name: Jostein Hodne
  • Contact Person: Njål Sørland

Our client is Jostein Hodne. He is an 18 year old musician based in Trondhiem, Norway. We were introduced to Jostein through one of our team members, Njål, who have known Jostein for x years. Jostein is an up and coming acoustic fingerstyle guitarist who already at an early age have establish himself in the acoustic guitar community. His goal is to spread his music to a more mainstream audience. We want to help Jostein reach his goal and will help him build a website where he can promote his music, announce future events and get in contact with his fanbase and other musicians.

Purpose and Goals

Our websites goal is to give Jostein a modern, accessible and x hub where he can show of his talents. We want to broadcast his music to a new and broader audience while maintaining the fans who already follow him. Thus, our goal for the website is to make it as easy as possible to discover, follow and contact Jostin. The website will be easy to navigate through a menu with every part of the website always accessible. We want to reach Jostein’s goals for the website through a home/news page, a biography, a showcase of his music and videos, upcoming events, and contact information.

Audience

The websites main audience will be Josteins fans who want to follow and keep up to date with the release of new music, upcoming events and social media. We also want to target a broader audience of music fans who might enjoy the music and talents of Jostein. The site will also be targeted at potential talent scouts who might have heard about Jostein’s skills and want to reach out to him.

Content of the Website and Organization

  1. "Home/news" This page will be the main page of the website. It will contain a small bio and a newsfeed where Jostein can publish news about upcoming music, events, videos and more. This will be where the fans can receive all the information they need at a glance. Our goal for this page is to make it simple, yet contain enough information without making it cluttered.
  2. "Biography" This page will contain a longer bio and description of who Jostein Hodne is, his goals, his accomplishments and pictures. This is where new fans and scouts will get to know who Jostein is and what he has accomplished so far in his career. The page will contain pictures from throughout his career.
  3. "Music" This page will contain all of Josteins songs. The page will be divided into different sections, each containing one of his earlier projects. There should be links to the songs on spotify and other music streaming platforms, as well as the ability to play small samples. The should also be a small description of the song, with Josteins own thoughts and notes for the individual song.
  4. "Events" This page will list all of Josteins upcoming events and concerts.
  5. "Contact This page will include Josteins contact info, as well as links to his social medias. There will also be a contact form where you can fill in your email-address and message, and automatically generate an email to Jostein.

Functional and nonfunctional requirements

Functional requirements:

  • The home page should always be reachable.
  • The different tabs mentioned above should always be visible from every part of the website.
  • The event page should include time, place and a link to ticket sale (if possible and accessible) for every event.
  • The biography page should include pictures as well as text.
  • The contact page should include an option to sign up for a newsletter.
  • The contact page should include direct links to all social media platforms.
  • The contact page should include an form to directly send a email to Jostein.
  • The music/video page should include in-browser support for Spotify.
  • The music/video page should display youtube videos directly at the page.

Non-functional requirements

  • The website should load in a reasonable time.
  • The website should be accessible on both laptops and mobile.
  • The website should be up-and-running 99.99% of the time.
  • The information on the biography page should be continuously updated, both text and photos.
  • The entire website should check most of the WCAG 2 guidelines.

Final Location of the Website

Jostein will host the website when the project is over.
